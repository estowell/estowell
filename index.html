Research Interests

My research is in the fields of Public Health, Human-Computer Interactio (HCI), and in Personal Health Informatics. I work with Dr. Andrea Parker in the

Wellness Technology Lab

. I am broadly interested in social determinants of sexual health and mental health, stigmatization of disease, global health and community development, and health activism. I am exploring how social media data can be used in identifying and addressing health disparities in sexual and mental health.
